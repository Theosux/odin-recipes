Carbonara

Kind of a "prequel" to the other recipe. (actually my fav food)

Pasta alla Carbonara is a dish with a popular tradition, made with simple and easily accessible ingredients; their origins are uncertain and regarding them there are several versions, of which the best known traces their origins to the region of Lazio and, in particular, to the city of Rome.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Cream
  • Bacon
  • More cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Place it in a pan
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Add the bacon
